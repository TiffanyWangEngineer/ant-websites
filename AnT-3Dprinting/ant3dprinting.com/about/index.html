Company

Our Story

AnT 3Dprinting was established in Alhambra, California in 2018 October. The company name stands for Alec and Tiffany, the co-founders of the AnT 3Dprinting company.

 

Our target is to be a high efficient and customer-friendly 3D printing company in the United States. We aim at providing economic friendly online 3D printing service for individual customers. Our company slogan is “Think a little more for the customers”.

Board of Directions

Alec Alejo    CEO

Alec Alejo is the Founder and CEO at AnT 3Dprinting, Inc. company. He has professional 3D printing field experience with customers and cutting-edge technologies.

Tiffany Wang   CFO

Tiffany Wang is the co-founder and CFO at AnT 3Dprinting, Inc. She got a master degree in Software Engineering from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University in Florida. She is professional on user experience and assiantant technologies for printing services.
